Damian Kuzovkov

It all started in 2011 when I worked as an alarm maintenance technician. I had a lot of free time and I thought what to do with it. One of my friends tried his hand at website development and after telling me alot about it, I was very interested. I found courses online and started learning. I tried it and I liked it) The technology stack was HTML, CSS, PHP. The first site was for my parents and was a catalog of their favorite recipes. The second one was ordered by my friend for their biker club. It was a simple site with basic information about the club and the events organized by the club, which was filled by me. For the full implementation of my plan, I set up an Apache server with a MySQL database with phpMyAdmin on my computer. Unfortunately, it was only a hobby, and when I was offered the position of technical director in a new branch, I agreed and there was no more time to develop and support sites. When in 2019 my family and I decided to start a new life and move to Prague I realized that I want to do what I really like. Since then I have started to learn modern web development. At the moment my tech stack is HTML, CSS, SASS entry level, JS , TS, React, Redux, REST API, some knowledge of Material-UI, AntDesign, Bootstrap. I continue to move on and at this stage I continue to practice JS, React, Redux .
